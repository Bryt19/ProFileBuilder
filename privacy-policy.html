Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2025

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when using our resume and cover letter builder services:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and other contact details you enter in your resume or cover letter
  • Resume Data: All information you input into our resume builder, including work experience, education, skills, and achievements
  • Cover Letter Data: Content you create using our cover letter generator
  • Profile Photos: Images you upload for your resume profile
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our services, including templates used and features accessed

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain our resume and cover letter building services
  • Save your progress and allow you to access your documents
  • Generate PDF exports of your resumes and cover letters
  • Improve our services and develop new features
  • Provide customer support and respond to your inquiries
  • Send important updates about our services

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored locally in your browser using localStorage. This means:

  • Your information never leaves your device
  • We don't have access to your personal data
  • Your data is automatically saved as you work
  • You can access your documents even when offline

Note: Since data is stored locally, clearing your browser data will remove your saved information. We recommend regularly exporting your documents as PDFs for backup.

Information Sharing

We do not share, sell, or trade your personal information with third parties. Since your data is stored locally on your device, we cannot access or share it.

The only exception is when you explicitly choose to export your resume or cover letter as a PDF, which creates a file on your device that you control.

Cookies and Tracking

Our service uses minimal cookies and tracking:

  • Theme Preference: We save your light/dark mode preference
  • Template Selection: We remember your chosen resume template
  • Color Scheme: We save your preferred color scheme

We do not use tracking cookies, analytics, or advertising cookies.

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: All your data is stored locally and accessible through our interface
  • Modify: You can edit or delete any information at any time
  • Export: You can download your documents as PDFs
  • Delete: You can clear all data by using the reset function or clearing browser data

Data Retention

Your data is stored locally in your browser until you:

  • Manually clear your browser data
  • Use the reset function in our application
  • Switch to a different browser or device

We recommend regularly backing up your documents by exporting them as PDFs.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Your continued use of our services after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
