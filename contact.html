Frequently Asked Questions

Is ResumeBuilder completely free to use?
Yes! All our resume and cover letter building features are completely free. There are no hidden fees or premium tiers.
How do I save my progress?
Your work is automatically saved to your browser's local storage as you type. You can also export your documents as PDFs for backup.
Are my resumes ATS-friendly?
Absolutely! All our templates are designed to be ATS (Applicant Tracking System) friendly with clean formatting and standard sections.
Can I use my own photos in my resume?
Yes, you can upload a profile photo to your resume. We support common image formats like JPG, PNG, and GIF.
What if I lose my data?
Since data is stored locally, we recommend regularly exporting your documents as PDFs. If you clear your browser data, your saved information will be lost.
Do you store my personal information?
No, we don't store your personal information on our servers. All data is stored locally in your browser using localStorage.
